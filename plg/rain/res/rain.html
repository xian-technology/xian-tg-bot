You can send XIAN to last active users in the group by specifying the total amount of XIAN to distribute, and the time frame in which users needed to write something in the group to be considered for the rain. The time frame can be in minutes or hours. ◾️ Rain 100 XIAN to users active in last 10 minutes /{{handle}} 100 10m If you want to rain some other token then you can do that too ◾️ Rain 500 DEGEN tokens to users active in last 3 hours /{{handle}} DEGEN 500 3h
