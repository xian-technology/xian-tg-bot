You can send XIAN or some other token to last active users in the current group by specifying the total amount of tokens to be distributed, and the time frame in which users needed to write a message in the group. The time frame can be in minutes or hours. Messages starting with "/" will be ignored! ◾️ Rain 100 XIAN to users active in last 10 minutes /{{handle}} 100 10m ◾️ Rain 100 BLUP tokens to users active in last 24 hours /{{handle}} blup 100 24h
