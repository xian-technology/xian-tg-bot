Start a lottery and deposit a certain amount of a token into a contract and every user that participates can in the end win the full amount When you deposit XIAN, you don't need to specify the contract or ticker. Just the amount is enough. For other tokens, you can use either the ticker or the contract name. ◾️ Create lottery, deposit 10 XIAN: /{{handle}} 10 ◾️ Create lottery, deposit 100 POOP using ticker: /{{handle}} poop 100 ◾️ Create lottery, deposit 100 POOP using contract: /{{handle}} con_poop_coin 100
