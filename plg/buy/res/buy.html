Buy tokens on the DEX. You can specify either a trading pair or just the token you want to buy. You can only use tokens that you set up for your account in the /tokens command. ◾️ Buy 10 XIAN (will use XUSDC): /{{handle}} xian 10 ◾️ Buy 10 BEER (will use XIAN): /{{handle}} beer 10 ◾️ Buy 10 XIAN for xUSDC (explicit pair): /{{handle}} xian-xusdc 10 ◾️ Buy 10 xUSDC for XIAN (explicit pair): /{{handle}} xusdc-xian 10
