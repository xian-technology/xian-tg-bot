Sell tokens on the DEX. You can specify either a trading pair or just the token you want to sell. You can only use tokens that you set up for your account in the /tokens command. ◾️ Sell 10 XIAN (will get XUSDC): /{{handle}} xian 10 ◾️ Sell 10 BEER (will get XIAN): /{{handle}} beer 10 ◾️ Sell 10 XIAN for xUSDC (explicit pair): /{{handle}} xian-xusdc 10 ◾️ Sell 10 xUSDC for XIAN (explicit pair): /{{handle}} xusdc-xian 10
